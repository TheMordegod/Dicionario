Pesquise a palavra

Word: Car

Type: noun

Description: a four-wheeled road vehicle that is powered by an engine and is able to carry a small number of people.

Origin: late Middle English (in the general sense ‘wheeled vehicle’): from Old Northern French carre, based on Latin carrum, carrus, of Celtic origin.

Exemples: "she drove up in a car"

Synonyms:
  • automobile
  • motor
  • machine
  • wheels
  • heap
  • crate
  • (old) banger
  • jalopy
  • limo
  • auto
  • hooptie
  • motor car
  • horseless carriage
